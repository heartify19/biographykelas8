Her Story

My mom, Afra Viena, is one of the inspiring people I know. She's the co-founder of SOVLO, a proudly Indonesian brand that turns local art into everyday fashion.

SOVLO stands for Souvenir Lokal, and it started during the pandemic in 2020 with a simple but powerful mission: to help local illustrators and tailors by transforming their art into wearable products like bags, clothes, accessories, and more. Sovlo isnt just a business, it's a creative movement. Every design is original, carefully curated, and made by Indonesian artists. My mom always says its about bringing art closer to people’s lives—and giving artists the platform they deserve. Today, Sovlo has grown massively, with 20 offline stores, 1.5 million+ products sold, and a workshop that supports 40+ skilled tailors. Theyve also worked with over 850 illustrators, some of whom now earn up to Rp10 million a month from their designs!

But beyond all her achievements, she's just my mom. She's fun, carefree, extroverted, easygoing, and honestly spoils me a lot. She's always surrounded by friends, family, full of energy (well, most of the time.), and thinks positively.

Her Wisdom

What I admire most is how she leads with heart. She's ambitious, believes in people, supportive and in doing things with purpose. No matter how busy she is, she will always sacrifice time for her family too.
